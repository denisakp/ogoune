# Licensing

Ogoune detects its edition at runtime from the `ENTERPRISE_LICENSE_KEY` environment variable.

| Prefix | Edition |
|---|---|
| _(unset)_ | Community |
| `pg_ent_…` | Enterprise |

Edition is **runtime metadata** — it identifies which build you're running for support and telemetry purposes.

## Community license

The Community Edition is licensed under **Apache 2.0**. You can self-host it freely.

## Enterprise license

The Enterprise Edition is under **LicenseRef-Ogoune-EE**. Contact us for a license key.
